# Privacy Policy for Qibla Compass Offline

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction

Qibla Compass Offline ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our services.

By using the Qibla Compass Offline app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Data (Precise Location)

**What We Collect:**

- GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
- Location accuracy information

**Why We Collect It:**

- **Qibla Direction:** To calculate the accurate direction to Kaaba (Mecca) from your current location
- **Prayer Times:** To determine accurate prayer times based on your geographical location
- **Local Prayer Times:** To provide prayer time calculations specific to your region

**How We Use It:**

- Location data is used in real-time for calculations
- Temporarily cached for offline functionality
- Shared with Prayer Times API (alquran.cloud) to fetch accurate prayer schedules

**User Control:**

- Location permission is required for the app to function
- You can revoke location permission in your device settings, but core features will not work

### 2. Device Identifiers

**What We Collect:**

- Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
- Android ID
- Device model and manufacturer information

**Why We Collect It:**

- **Advertising:** To serve personalized advertisements through Google AdMob and InMobi
- **Analytics:** To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience
- **Ad Performance:** To measure ad effectiveness and optimize ad delivery

**How We Use It:**

- Shared with Google AdMob and InMobi for ad serving
- Used for analytics to improve app performance
- Never used to identify you personally

**User Control:**

- You can reset your Advertising ID in device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID)
- You can opt out of personalized ads (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization)

### 3. App Activity and Interactions

**What We Collect:**

- App screens visited
- Features used (Qibla compass, Prayer times, Quran reading, etc.)
- Button clicks and user interactions
- Time spent in the app

**Why We Collect It:**

- **Analytics:** To understand which features are most popular
- **App Improvement:** To identify areas for enhancement
- **Ad Optimization:** To show relevant advertisements

**How We Use It:**

- Analyzed to improve app functionality
- Shared with advertising networks for targeted ads
- Used to fix bugs and optimize performance

### 4. App Performance Data

**What We Collect:**

- **Crash Logs:** Error reports when the app crashes
- **Diagnostics:** App performance metrics (load times, memory usage)
- App version and device specifications

**Why We Collect It:**

- **Bug Fixes:** To identify and resolve technical issues
- **Performance Optimization:** To improve app speed and stability
- **Quality Assurance:** To ensure the app works across different devices

**How We Use It:**

- Analyzed internally to fix bugs
- Used to optimize app performance
- Not shared with third parties (except crash reporting services)

## Information We DO NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect:

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- ❌ Financial information (credit cards, payment details)
- ❌ Photos, videos, or audio files from your device
- ❌ Contact lists
- ❌ SMS or call logs
- ❌ Web browsing history
- ❌ Account credentials or passwords
- ❌ Health or fitness information

## Data Security

We take data security seriously:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** Locally stored data is encrypted using secure storage methods
- **Limited Access:** Only authorized services have access to necessary data
- **No Permanent Storage:** Location data is not permanently stored on our servers

## Data Retention and Deletion

### Automatic Deletion

- **Location Data:** Used in real-time, not permanently stored
- **Cache Data:** Automatically deleted after 30-90 days
- **App Activity:** Analytics data is retained for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted
- **Crash Logs:** Retained for 90 days for bug fixing purposes

### Manual Deletion

To delete app data:

1. **Reset Advertising ID:** Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
2. **Clear App Data:** Settings → Apps → Qibla Compass Offline → Clear Data
3. **Uninstall App:** Removes all local data immediately

### Account Deletion

Our app does not require account creation, so there are no accounts to delete.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The data we collect (location for Qibla direction, device IDs for ads) is the minimum necessary for app functionality and is handled with strict privacy controls.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Right to Access

You can view data stored locally on your device through:

- Android Settings → Apps → Qibla Compass Offline → Storage

### Right to Delete

You have the right to delete all app data:

- Clear app data in device settings
- Uninstall the app
- Reset your Advertising ID

### Right to Opt-Out of Ads

You can limit ad tracking:

- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

### Right to Revoke Permissions

You can control app permissions:
